Crop Yield Prediction

Machine learning models to predict growth of crops in the United States

1) Implemented 5 regression models namely - Multivariate Linear Regression, Shrinkage methods (Ridge and Lasso regression), k-nearest neighbors, Decision Tree and Artificial Neural Networks to predict crop yield
